About SDE

Salon De Elegance is a one-stop shop for all of your hair and skin care needs. It is well-known for offering high-quality services such as haircuts, hair colouring, manicures, pedicures, waxing, and a variety of facials and makeovers done by trained professionals. With hairstylists that strive to exceed your expectations, the team ensures their clients are always number one. Salon De Elegance is the place to go if you want a simple yet stylish personalised haircut. Hairstylists also make recommendations based on your personality to make you look and feel your best. It is open seven days a week and is located on Hessargatta main road in Bangalore. So come in whenever you want to get the look of your dreams!
